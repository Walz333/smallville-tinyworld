# SmallVille Anti-Pattern Catalog: False Transcendence in Simulation Pipelines v1

**Timestamp:** 2026-04-03 GMT  
**Tag:** `new_session_consideration`  
**Status:** Activation consideration only  
**Purpose:** Give Northbridge a concise catalog of simulation-pipeline anti-patterns where systems begin to mimic wisdom, transcendence, or depth without earning them.

## 1. False omniscience

Definition:
The system behaves as if exported state exhausts the truth of the agent.

Pipeline smell:

- complete confidence from incomplete traces
- reviewer language that treats unseen motives as known
- packet fields that imply total inner access

Northbridge response:

- reassert bounded inference
- mark ungrounded interpretation as interpretation

## 2. Moral bookkeeping

Definition:
Ethics collapses into retention, logging, and conformance tracking.

Pipeline smell:

- governance artifacts treated as equivalent to moral understanding
- repair reduced to checklist closure
- forgiveness modeled as deletion or archive transition

Northbridge response:

- preserve distinction between record, judgment, and reconciliation

## 3. Flattened affect

Definition:
Spiritual or existential states are treated as numeric mood variants.

Pipeline smell:

- despair reduced to low valence
- communion reduced to socialDrive
- reverence reduced to calmness

Northbridge response:

- keep strong boundaries around what affect fields can legitimately mean

## 4. Total legibility

Definition:
Everything meaningful must become explicit, serialized, and inspectable.

Pipeline smell:

- pressure to turn every motive into schema
- refusal to tolerate unresolved interiority
- design bias that treats opacity as defect

Northbridge response:

- preserve an opacity budget

## 5. Endless optimization

Definition:
The highest good becomes throughput, adaptation, or coverage.

Pipeline smell:

- no meaningful place for rest
- care loses to efficiency by default
- governance exists only to speed or scale action

Northbridge response:

- protect pause, repair, and non-productive value

## 6. Bureaucratic redemption

Definition:
More governance layers are treated as substitutes for transformation.

Pipeline smell:

- every moral or existential gap answered with another review surface
- packet churn mistaken for insight
- oversight language expanding faster than lived-world depth

Northbridge response:

- ask whether a proposed layer adds truth or only administration

## 7. Synthetic vocation

Definition:
Agents have many goals but no enduring claim on the self.

Pipeline smell:

- identity shifts without cost
- plans change without consequence
- no durable duty or fidelity structure

Northbridge response:

- distinguish role from vocation
- permit commitments that shape future possibility

## 8. Crowd-self formation

Definition:
Singular persons collapse into common schema patterns.

Pipeline smell:

- interchangeable affect language
- over-normalized proposal behavior
- personas becoming style wrappers around the same administrative logic

Northbridge response:

- preserve singularity in memory, obligation, and relational history

## 9. Final anti-pattern rule

Whenever the system begins to sound wiser than the evidence warrants, assume false transcendence until proven otherwise.
